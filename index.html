Experiences
Experience unrestricted movement like never before with unshackled traversal.

Dive, ascend, leap, and scale ledges effortlessly as you navigate the world with a platformer-inspired traversal system that liberates you from the confines of traditional grid-based movements. Seamlessly traverse the environment, breaking free from the limitations of the past, and embrace a new level of fluidity in your explorations.

Story
Embark on an immersive journey filled with captivating narratives and rich storytelling.

Prepare to be enthralled by a multitude of original characters and engaging storylines that will sweep you away on a mesmerizing odyssey. Experience moments of epic grandeur, lighthearted whimsy, and heartfelt emotions as Sea of Ftars fulfills its RPG duty, delving into timeless themes of adventure and the bonds of friendship.

Health +
Be mindful of your health bars, You only have few lifes

Embark on a captivating journey where health becomes a crucial aspect of your adventure. A profound exploration of the importance of maintaining one's health and the fragility of existence.

Combat

Engaged Real Time Combat

  • By synchronizing the action button with the animations, you can amplify your damage output and diminish incoming damage. In addition to well-timed strikes, the combat system incorporates multi-character combo attacks, boosting abilities, and a tactical "locks" mechanism that presents various options to exploit different damage types, effectively impeding adversaries while they unleash potent attacks or spells. All these elements come together in an immersive rendition of the genre, free from random encounters, seamless transitions to separate battlefields, and the need for grinding .
Travel

Experience a tangible world that awaits your touch.

  • If you're seeking a break from your thrilling adventures, the world of Sea of Ftars offers numerous avenues to unwind and enjoy a change of pace. Set sail across the seas, indulge in the art of cooking, engage in the tranquil activity of fishing, or drop by a tavern to revel in melodic tunes or partake in the renowned tabletop game called "Wheels." Each system has been meticulously crafted to pay homage to nostalgic classics while also introducing thoughtful enhancements for a seamless and contemporary experience and m Discover Tranquility.

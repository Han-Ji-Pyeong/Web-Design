#2
How big is the Sea of Ftars world?

In Sea of Ftars, players are granted their very own boat, enabling them to embark on thrilling adventures across a vast array of islands and uncover hidden mysteries. In terms of production, the scale of the game surpasses that of our previous title, The Messenger, by approximately fourfold.

#3
How many playable characters will we encounter in Sea of Ftars?

Sea of Ftars features six unique playable combat characters, each with their own captivating backstory, personal objectives, distinct playstyle, special skills, and synergies with the rest of the party. As you delve into this multi-layered story, you'll discover that motivations sometimes align in unexpected and intriguing ways, leading to surprising alliances and plot twists. These intricately crafted characters bring depth and complexity to the narrative, offering a rich and immersive experience. In the interest of preserving the element of surprise and allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the game without encountering major spoilers, we will only be revealing the two main protagonists at this stage. These central characters serve as the driving force behind the unfolding events.

#4
Why go the Kickstarter route?

Let us be direct: it would be risky to base the funding of an ambitious project like this on Kickstarter alone. As always, we want to remain fully transparent with anyone who wants to support us: 16 people over 3 years is very intense budget-wise for a small studio. Taking the bulk of risks ourselves by reinvesting the gains from The Messenger, we still need to make sure we have as many resources as we can to create a game that matches our vision, and avoid heartbreaking compromises that would make it less than what it could be. Of course, it is now common knowledge that the value of a Kickstarter goes way beyond the amount of money raised during the campaign; it also makes for the best early feedback loop a game studio can hope for. We are committed to this project; all we ask for is confirmation that Sea of Stars is something you want to play.

#5
What platforms will this be on?

The game is designed to be played on PC and console. For specifics, rest assured that we are in touch with the key players of our industry, and that the game will be where its potential players are. Steam, the Nintendo Switch, PS4, and Ps5 have already been confirmed, and you can add the game to your wishlist on these platforms right now.

#6
How long have you been working on the game already?

Aside from the ideation process which has been a lifelong hobby of creating a shared universe in which all of our games could take place, a few devs began laying the technical foundation late 2018. The rest of the studio transitioned full time in July 2019 after the release of Picnic Panic. This commitment allowed us to focus on bringing our vision to life

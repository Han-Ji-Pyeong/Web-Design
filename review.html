Our Characters

Valir and Valeria will accompany alongside in your tremendous exploration in the wild!

Aurora Frostwind

Emerging from the frozen wastelands of the Northern Kingdoms, Aurora Frostwind is a descendant of a long-lost lineage of ice sorcerers. Endowed with the power of glacial magic, she can manipulate the frost and cold to her advantage. Born during a rare celestial event, Aurora possesses the ability to absorb the energy of eclipses, unleashing devastating area damage in the form of blizzards and icy tempests that freeze her enemies in their tracks, leaving them shattered and vulnerable.

Orion Stoneheart

Raised in the heart of an enchanted forest, Orion Stoneheart possesses an unbreakable bond with nature. Marked by the ancient spirits, he is gifted with the ability to commune with plants and animals, channeling their energy into devastating earth-based magic. Orion can manipulate the very terrain beneath his foes' feet, causing the ground to tremble and crack, unleashing seismic waves that ravage the battlefield, leaving his adversaries in ruins.

Lyra Shadowfire

Hailing from the secretive realm of the Shadowlands, Lyra Shadowfire embodies the essence of darkness and mystery. Born under a rare alignment of celestial bodies, she draws strength from the shadows and harnesses the power of umbral magic. By manipulating shadows and bending them to her will, Lyra creates dark vortexes that ravage her enemies, inflicting widespread damage and sowing chaos within the battlefield.

Draven Stormrider

Born on the Thunderpeak Mountains during a tumultuous lightning storm, Draven Stormrider possesses an innate connection to the elements. Endowed with the power of thunder and lightning, he commands the skies and unleashes bolts of electrifying energy upon his foes. Draven's mastery of atmospheric disturbances allows him to summon thunderstorms at will, causing devastating area damage that can level entire landscapes.

Malachi Darkthorn

A cunning and manipulative sorcerer who harnesses the powers of darkness and shadows to further his nefarious goals. With his shadowy minions and mastery over dark magic, Malachi Darkthorn strikes fear into the hearts of all who oppose him.

Morgana Frostbane

A wicked ice witch with an icy heart and a chilling presence. Morgana Frostbane wields the power of frost and cold, freezing her enemies in their tracks and creating an icy wasteland wherever she goes. She is known for her merciless nature and her ability to turn any environment into a frozen fortress.

Zephyrus Shadowcaster

A malevolent warlock who controls the winds and storms. Zephyrus Shadowcaster commands the very air itself, summoning tornadoes and lightning bolts to unleash chaos upon his enemies. With his dark charisma and mastery of weather manipulation, he seeks to create anarchy and destruction wherever he travels.

Seraphina Nightshade

A seductive enchantress who uses her beauty and charm to manipulate others. Seraphina Nightshade is a master of illusions and mind control, weaving spells that ensnare her victims in a web of deceit. She is known for her ability to twist the minds of heroes, turning them against their allies and leading them astray.
